# Product Requirements Document (PRD) — KaziX

## 1. Vision & Goals
KaziX is a modern marketplace designed to connect skilled Kenyan workers (fundis) with clients. The platform prioritizes security and trust through built-in M-Pesa escrow payments, ID verification, and a transparent review system.

**Goals:**
- Provide a reliable platform for hiring verified fundis in Kenya.
- Ensure payment security for both clients and workers using M-Pesa escrow.
- Maintain high service quality through a transparent review and rating system.
- Achieve fast hiring times (target: under 8 minutes).

## 2. Target Audience
- **Clients:** Homeowners or businesses in Kenya needing repair, maintenance, or manual labor services (plumbers, electricians, painters, etc.).
- **Fundis (Pros):** Skilled manual workers looking for job opportunities in their local areas.

## 3. Key Features
- **ID Verification:** All fundis must have verified IDs to build trust.
- **M-Pesa Escrow:** Payments are held securely and only released upon job completion confirmation.
- **SMS Job Alerts:** Real-time notifications for fundis when relevant jobs are posted.
- **Review System:** Transparent feedback from clients to help others make informed decisions.
- **Job Dashboard:** Dedicated areas for both clients and workers to manage their active and past jobs.

## 4. Technical Requirements
- **Frontend:** HTML5, Vanilla CSS3 (modern features like CSS Variables, Grid, Flexbox).
- **Design Aesthetic:** High-contrast "Modern Brutalist" style.
- **Typography:** `Syne` for headings, `DM Sans` for body text.
- **Backend:** Node.js environment (initial scaffolding being set up).
- **Mobile-First:** Prioritizing accessibility and usability on mobile devices.

## 5. Success Metrics
- Average response time of under 8 minutes.
- High percentage of jobs completed through the escrow system.
- Positive ratings and reviews for verified fundis.
